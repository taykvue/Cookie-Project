Ingredients

The main ingredients listed below was used to make the cookie dough for the coconut turtle matcha cookie and the filling base ingredients to make the core fillings. The filling base ingredient can be customized based on the flavor you like. For this cookie recipe, the filling consisted of a cocoa filling core and the sweet purple potato filling core cover.

Main Ingredients

  • 1 large white egg
  • 1 ¼ cups of bread flour
  • 1 tablespoon ginger crystal
  • ½ cup of unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoon green tea matcha powder
  • ½ cup of brown sugar
  • ¼ cup of white sugar
  • 2 tablespoon honey
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon coconut flakes

Filling Ingredients

  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoon honey
  • ¼ cup coconut milk
  • ½ tablespoon lemon zest
  • ½ tablespoon orange zest
  • 8 dried medjool dates, warmed, pitted
  • 6 table spoon cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon purple sweet potato powder
